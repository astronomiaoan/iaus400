MedellínColombia
City of Eternal Spring
The symposium would take place at the heart of Colombia’s coffee region, in Medellín, a city that has recently obtained multiple prizes for urban development and innovation (International Smart City prize 2020, Lee Kuan Yew Works City prize 2016, Veronica Rudge Green 2013 award, Holcim Award Gold 2008) thanks to the dramatic transformation it has experienced in the last 20 years, now becoming a major hub for academic studies, entrepreneurship and tourism.

The conclusion of the symposium is timed with the start of one of Colombia's most significant celebrations, the Feria de las Flores (Flower Festival). Participants will therefore have the possibility to experience the vibrant tapestry of Medellín during the Feria de las Flores from 26 July to 5 August, immersing in the breathtaking displays of meticulously crafted flower arrangements, transforming the city into a living garden.

Before and after the Festival, participants will enjoy lively cultural performances, traditional dances, and local cuisine, creating an immersive experience that captivates the senses.
