Frequently Asked Questions
Event Venue: Fundadores Auditorium, EAFIT University
The event will be held at the Fundadores Auditorium, one of the premier event spaces on the EAFIT University campus, located in the heart of Medellín, Colombia. This modern auditorium is equipped with state-of-the-art audio and visual systems, comfortable seating for a large audience, and an elegant design that creates a welcoming atmosphere for conferences, presentations, and cultural events.

Capacity: The auditorium can accommodate up to 760 attendees, with excellent acoustics and visibility from all seating areas, ensuring that every participant has an optimal experience.

About EAFIT University
EAFIT University is one of the most prestigious institutions in Colombia, known for its innovation, academic excellence, and vibrant campus life. Founded in 1960, EAFIT has grown into a dynamic educational and cultural hub in Medellín, offering a range of undergraduate, graduate, and research programs. The campus is renowned for its green spaces, modern architecture, and rich cultural offerings, making it an ideal venue for both academic and professional events.

Facilities and Resources
Participants will have access to a variety of resources and amenities across the EAFIT campus, including:

Libraries: The university's main library offers a wide range of books, digital resources, and quiet study areas.
Computer Labs: Equipped with the latest software and high-speed internet for any technical needs.
Green Spaces: EAFIT is known for its beautiful gardens and green areas, perfect for relaxing or networking between sessions.

Dining Options EAFIT offers several restaurants and cafés on campus that cater to a wide range of tastes and dietary needs. Some popular spots include:
La Plaza Café: A cafeteria with a variety of local and international dishes, offering quick meals and snacks.
Ágora Café: A more relaxed coffee shop setting with gourmet coffee, pastries, and light meals.
La Cafetería Central: A student-favorite with multiple food options, including vegetarian and healthy choices.
Off-campus, the university is located near El Poblado, a vibrant neighborhood full of excellent dining options, from traditional Colombian cuisine to international gourmet experiences. Restaurants and cafés around this area include a mix of fine dining, casual eateries, and coffee shops.

Transportation and Accessibility
EAFIT University is centrally located, making it easy to access via various modes of transportation. The Poblado Metro Station is just a short walk from the campus, and there are numerous bus routes and taxis available in the area. Parking is also available on campus for those driving to the event.

Nearby Attractions
For participants looking to explore Medellín after the event, the university is close to several key attractions, including:

El Tesoro Shopping Center: A popular shopping mall with a variety of retail, dining, and entertainment options.
Parque Lleras: A bustling nightlife and dining area, known for its vibrant atmosphere and excellent restaurants.
Museo de Arte Moderno de Medellín: A contemporary art museum showcasing both Colombian and international artists, just a short drive from the campus.
