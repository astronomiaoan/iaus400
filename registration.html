Abstract Submission

As part of your participation in the IAU Symposium 400, we invite you to submit abstracts for both oral and poster presentations. This is an excellent opportunity to share your research with a global community of scientists, discuss the latest developments in solar and stellar physics, and receive valuable feedback from experts in the field. The abstract submission portal will open on December 15, 2024, and the deadline for submissions is February 28, 2025. Submissions will be reviewed by the Scientific Organizing Committee, and selected contributions will be announced by March 30, 2025. Please ensure that your abstract adheres to the symposium’s guidelines, which will be available on this page once submissions are open.



Registration

Registration for the symposium will also open on December 15, 2024. Early registration is highly encouraged to secure your spot at this event, which promises to be an unparalleled gathering of leading researchers and professionals in solar and stellar physics. You will have the option to register at a reduced rate if you complete your registration before the Early Registration Deadline on February 28, 2025. After this date, regular registration fees will apply. Please note that registration will close on April 20, 2025, so we recommend registering early to take full advantage of all the symposium benefits, including access to all sessions, materials, and social events.



Student grants

We are committed to supporting the next generation of scientists, and student travel grants will be available for eligible participants. Students who wish to apply for financial assistance must complete the application form by January 25, 2025. These grants are designed to assist with travel, registration, and accommodation costs, allowing students to fully engage with the symposium’s sessions, networking opportunities, and scientific discussions. When applying, be sure to provide all required documentation, including proof of student status and a short statement outlining the relevance of your research to the symposium themes. The final notification of grant recipients will be made by March 30, 2025. We strongly encourage all students to apply for this valuable opportunity.





For any questions regarding registration, abstract submissions, or student assistance, please feel free to contact our team through the Contact page. We are here to help ensure your participation in this exciting event is as smooth as possible.
