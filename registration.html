Abstract Submission

As part of your participation in the IAU Symposium 400, we invite you to submit abstracts for both oral and poster presentations. This is an excellent opportunity to share your research with a global community of scientists, discuss the latest developments in solar and stellar physics, and receive valuable feedback from experts in the field.
The abstract submission portal will open on October 21, 2024, and the deadline for submissions is January 21, 2025 (oral) and March 21, 2025 (poster).
Submissions will be reviewed by the Scientific Organizing Committee, and selected contributions will be announced by March 21, 2025 (oral) and Abril 21, 2025 (poster).

Please ensure that your abstract adheres to the symposium’s guidelines, which will be available on this page once submissions are open.






IAU Travel Grants

The IAU is committed to supporting the next generation of scientists, and travel grants will be available for eligible participants. Graduate students and early-career researchers who wish to apply for financial assistance must complete the application form by January 21, 2025.
These grants are designed to partially assist with travel, registration, and accommodation costs, allowing students to fully engage with the symposium’s sessions, networking opportunities, and scientific discussions. .
To be eligible for the grants, candidates must have an accepted contribution. It is recommended that participants applying for the Travel Grant submit the abstract of their contribution as soon as possible in order to receive a response regarding the acceptance of their work before the deadline to apply for the Travel Grant. The final notification of grant recipients will be made by March 21, 2025. We strongly encourage all students to apply for this valuable opportunity.






Registration

Registration for the symposium will open on January 21, 2024. Early registration is highly encouraged to secure your spot at this event, which promises to be an unparalleled gathering of leading researchers and professionals in solar and stellar physics.
You will have the option to register at a reduced rate if you complete your registration before the Early Registration Deadline on April 21, 2025. After this date, regular registration fees will apply. Please note that registration will close on May 21, 2025, so we recommend registering early to take full advantage of all the symposium benefits, including access to all sessions, materials, and social events.





For any questions regarding registration, abstract submissions, or student assistance, please feel free to contact our team through the Contact page. We are here to help ensure your participation in this exciting event is as smooth as possible.
